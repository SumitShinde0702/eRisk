# Cluster Playbooks

## AcuteSafety
- Primary goal: clarify intent, plan concreteness, timeline, means access, protective factors.
- Keep focus in this cluster until acute ladder coverage is sufficient.
- Avoid drifting to sleep/appetite first when imminent cues are present.

## HopelessWorthless
- Probe fatalism, self-worth collapse, "nothing matters" cognition.
- Watch for transition into AcuteSafety if imminence appears.

## CoreDepression
- Probe low mood frequency/intensity and anhedonia progression.
- Clarify social withdrawal and daily impact.

## VegetativeCognitive
- Probe sleep, energy, appetite, concentration, decision difficulty.
- Link these symptoms to function impairment.

## BehavioralArousal
- Probe agitation, irritability, restlessness and interpersonal impact.

## GeneralCheckin
- Use broad open-ended opening and then route to dominant risk/symptom cluster.
